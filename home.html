ABOUT US

Cannella's Italian Restaurant is family owned. Robert and Shelly Cannella opened the restaurant's doors in 1980. Everything on the menu is prepared cooked to order. We use the freshest hometown indgredients, and it makes our food great. Find time to bring the family in to Cannella's whether it be for lunch or dinner. We are open Monday-Friday: 12pm-10pm and Saturday-Sunday: 11am-11pm.
